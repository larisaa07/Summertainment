Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Over the summer I decided to reread one of my favorite book series. Every once in a while I like to go back to my old favorite books and read them again as a reminder. Every time I reread the Percy Jackson books I love that I see more and more foreshadowing. Some things that the characters say hint at what will happen later on in the series. Rereading is also great because you don't have to commit to a new story.
