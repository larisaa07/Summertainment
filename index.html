I spent my summer in California and it was an amazing experience. The weather was perfect every day and there was always something to do. I visited the beaches in Santa Monica and Malibu, went hiking in the mountains, and explored the city of Los Angeles. One of my favorite memories was visiting the Hollywood Bowl with my cousin and sister. The food in California was also incredible, with fresh fruits and vegetables available everywhere. Overall, my summer in California was unforgettable and I can't wait to go back and explore more of the state.
